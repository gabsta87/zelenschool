Ukraine Reborn association

We are an Independent Humanitarian Support Association that has started from a little collection point in Swiss Romande in the first week of war in Ukraine.

In just 3 weeks we have been able to collect more then 1000 boxes of humanitarian help that was sent to Ukraine and helped over 20 families fleeing to Switzerland.

We have seen and personally were touched by how stressed and lost refugees are, and how our small actions are making a big difference. Now, we are elevating our efforts uniting with our allies for the good cause.
